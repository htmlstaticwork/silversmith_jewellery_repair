Expert Services

Precision restoration and artisanal care for your timeless silver treasures, from heritage polishing to master-level repairs.

Jewelry Repair

We fix broken chains, mend clasps, and restructure damaged pieces so they can be worn safely once again. Each repair is treated to be invisible to the naked eye.

  • Chain Soldering
  • Clasp Replacement
  • Prong Retipping
  • Restringing

Silver Polishing

Over time, silver tarnishes and loses luster. We use professional-grade compounds and ultrasonic cleaning to carefully remove oxidation and restore a brilliant mirror finish.

  • Deep Ultrasonic Cleaning
  • Tarnish Removal
  • High-Gloss Buffing
  • Protective Coating

Ring Resizing

Whether it's sizing up or down, we adjust rings perfectly to your finger size without compromising the structural integrity or the beauty of the band.

  • Seamless Sizing Up/Down
  • Shank Replacement
  • Engraving Preservation

Stone Replacement

Lost a diamond or colored gemstone? We source and expertly set matching replacement stones, ensuring a secure and beautiful fit.

  • Diamond Sourcing
  • Color Gemstone Matching
  • Bezel & Prong Setting
